Welcome to the Setup of the Digitizer

This is the first time you started the digitizer after the initial installation. You have been automatically routed to the initial setup procedure. Here you adjust the settings for your meter within five steps. In the final step the inital setup will be disabled and it will restart to the normal mode. All settings will also be accessible there. See https://jomjol.github.io/AI-on-the-edge-device-docs/initial-setup for additional explanations.

You can navigate forward and backward during the setup with the buttons "Next" and "Previous".

Do not forget to save in each step before heading to another step!

This is an overview over the five steps:

  1. Create the Reference Image.
    It is the base for the position referencing and the identification of the digits and counters.

  2. Define two unique Reference Marks.
    They is used to align the individual camera images and identify the absolut positions.

  3. Define ROI's for the Digits.
    They will be used to digitize the digit part of your meter.
    If your meter has no digits, this step can be skipped.

  4. Define ROI's> for the Analog Counters.
    They will be used to digitize the analog part of your meter.
    If your meter has no analog counters, this step can be skipped.

  5. General Settings
    Further configuration of your device.

Please be patient when switching to another step. The device takes some time to load all needed information!

After step 5 the setup is completed, you then can reboot and starts into the normal operation mode.

If you need support, have a look to the documentation or the discussion pages.

Have fun with your digitizer!
